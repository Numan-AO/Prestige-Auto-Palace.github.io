Sell Your Car to Prestige Auto Palace

Looking to sell your car quickly and easily? Prestige Auto Palace is here to help!

At Prestige Auto Palace, we offer a hassle-free and straightforward process to sell your vehicle. Whether you're looking to upgrade to a new car or simply need to sell your current vehicle, we're committed to providing you with a fair and competitive offer.

Why Sell Your Car to Us?

Competitive Offers

We provide top dollar for your vehicle. Our team of experts evaluates your car thoroughly to ensure you receive a fair market value offer.

Fast and Easy Process

Our streamlined process makes selling your car quick and easy. From the moment you contact us to the final sale, we handle all the paperwork and details.

No Obligation

Get a free, no-obligation quote for your vehicle. If you decide to accept our offer, we’ll finalize the transaction quickly. If not, you’re free to explore other options.

How It Works

1. Get Your Free Quote

Visit our dealership for an in-person evaluation. If you can email us at sell@prestigeautopalace.com with details about your vehicle, including make, model, year, mileage, and condition.

2. Vehicle Inspection

Once we receive your information, our team will conduct a thorough inspection of your vehicle. This includes checking its condition, features, and any modifications.

3. Receive an Offer

After the inspection, we'll provide you with a competitive offer based on the current market value of your car. Our offers are transparent and fair.

4. Get Paid

If you accept our offer, we’ll handle all the paperwork and make the payment. You can choose to receive your payment via check or direct deposit.

What to Bring

To make the process smooth and efficient, please bring the following items when you come to sell your car:

Vehicle title

Valid ID (driver's license or passport)

All keys and remotes

Maintenance records (if available)
